How was this number calculated?

It uses a predictive analytics model based on library holdings of 5313 tattoo books published since 1850 that come up in a general keyword search for the word stem "tattoo*" in the WorldCat global archive. If you are interested in the nerdy, nitty-gritty details, you can find them here.
